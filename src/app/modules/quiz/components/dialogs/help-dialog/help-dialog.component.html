1 - Score
2 - Timer
3 - Restart the quiz
4 - Settings (number of attempts available and time)
5 - Help
6 - Dark/Light mode toggle
For each flag quiz participant is asked to match its corresponding country name, capital and also location on the map. There are two forms to type country name and capital name - one for each. To check the answer from the form press 'Enter'. To open the map press the button with globe icon on it.
To complete the 'map-quiz' user needs to click on the country matching the flag. Correct answer will close the map. Incorrect answers will apear as red areas.
Map can be moved by dragging and zoomed in/out using scroll.
